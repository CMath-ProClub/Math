Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can individual students register for school contests?
School-based contests (High School, Grades 4–8, Algebra 1) must be administered through a school. If your school doesn't participate, ask a math teacher or coach to register. Individuals can participate directly in the Online Summer Challenge and the International Summer Tournament.
How many students can participate per school?
There is no limit on the number of students who can participate. Each school receives a contest set that can be administered to as many students as desired. Only the top scores are reported for national rankings.
When is the deadline to register?
For High School contests, you can register at any point during the season and receive materials for remaining contests. For Grade School contests, registration typically closes a few weeks before the contest date. Contact us for specific deadlines.
Do you ship contest materials internationally?
Yes! Math League contests are used in over 6 countries. International schools can register and have materials shipped to their location. Additional shipping fees may apply. Email us for international pricing details.
What payment methods do you accept?
Schools can pay by check or purchase order. The Online Store accepts major credit cards for individual purchases such as contest books and sample contests. Contact us for invoicing details.
