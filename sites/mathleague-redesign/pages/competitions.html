Grades 4 & 5

Elementary School Contests

Designed to spark curiosity and build foundational problem-solving skills, our 4th and 5th grade contests are single annual events. Each contest covers age-appropriate topics from across mathematics, encouraging young students to think creatively and build confidence.

Format: One annual contest per grade level

Registration deadline: January 31, 2026

Materials ship: March

Contest date: April 15 or later

High School

High School Contests

Our most comprehensive program features six contests throughout the academic year, each covering different areas of mathematics. Schools compete in statewide league standings with results published monthly.

Format: Six contests per year (October–March)

Registration deadline: September 30, 2025

Alternate dates: Available one week after each contest date
