How It Works

Getting started is simple

1

Register Your School

Schools register online and select their desired contest levels. Individual students participate through their schools.

2

Compete in Contests

Students take contests at their schools on scheduled dates. Contests can be administered in-person or online.

3

Track Results

Results are published online with newsletters. Top performers may qualify for the International Summer Tournament.

For Educators

Why teachers choose Math League

When deciding which math competition is right for your students, here's what sets us apart.

Unbeatable Value

At $45 per set of 30, that's just $1.50 per student. High school gets six full contests for only $100 — less than $0.56 per student per contest.

Expert-Authored Problems

Every problem is crafted by Presidential Award recipients for Excellence in Mathematics Teaching. 48 years of proven, high-quality content.

Students Love It

Over 1 million students participate annually. Many discover a lifelong passion for mathematics through these contests. The problems are challenging but accessible — students feel genuine excitement when they crack a tough one.

Measurable Growth

Students track progress across six monthly contests (high school) or year over year. Teachers see tangible improvement in problem-solving skills that transfer to standardized tests and coursework.

Simple for Teachers

We ship everything ready to go — 30 copies per set. Administer at your school on your schedule. Online options available. No venue booking or travel required.

University Connections

Top performers can attend our summer tournament with lectures from professors at Princeton, Columbia, Rutgers, and more — including a Fields Medal winner.

"Math League contests give my students something to look forward to each month. They walk in nervous and walk out proud — even when they don't get every problem right. That's what real mathematical growth looks like."

— Math Department Faculty, High School Math League Coach
