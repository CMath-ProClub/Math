Frequently Asked Questions

How do I qualify for the World Finals?
Top-performing teams at WMC Regional Qualifiers earn a Golden Ticket ‚Äî a direct invitation to the World Finals. The number of Golden Tickets varies by regional, typically awarded to the top 2‚Äì4 teams.
What does the fee include?
The fee covers 7 nights of university campus accommodation, all meals, competition materials, cultural excursions, the awards ceremony, official WMC merchandise, and certificates. International flights are not included.
Do teams need a supervising adult?
Yes, each school team must be accompanied by at least one teacher or adult supervisor. Supervisor accommodation and meals are included in the team fee.
How many rounds are there at the Finals?
The World Finals feature 14 rounds across all five skill categories ‚Äî Challenge, Collaboration, Communication, Pure Rigour, and Application. Rounds are spread across the week with rest days and excursions in between.
Can we attend if we don't have a Golden Ticket?
In some cases, additional places may be available for teams that narrowly missed a Golden Ticket. Contact the WMC team to discuss availability and waitlist options.
