Philosophy

Mathematics as a Gift

The WMC believes that mathematics is a gift to be developed and shared with others. Through competition we gain confidence, and through collaboration, we achieve the extraordinary.

Unlike traditional math competitions that focus solely on individual problem-solving speed, the WMC assesses students across five distinct skill categories, ensuring a holistic development of mathematical thinking.

Every round is carefully designed to test different facets of mathematical ability ‚Äî from pure rigour and logic to creative application and teamwork. Students leave the WMC as better thinkers, communicators, and collaborators.
